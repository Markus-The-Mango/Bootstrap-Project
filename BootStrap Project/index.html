Top Thrill

January 10th, 2023, One day after Dragsters 20-year anniversery of being announced Cedar Point announced that the ride would get a retheme. The park hinted at a formula one theme saying the ride would get a "new formula for thrills".

Flat Rides

Flat rides can be defined as non roller coaster rides, like scramblers, drop towers, and ferris wheels. For me flat rides are a perfect way to enjoy time between riding larger rides, and the smaller size makes some of them ideal to travel.

Operations

Roller coasters, flat rides and literally everything in a park would not exist without help from the amazing workers who do their jobs so others can have fun. I applaud not only, ride operators and attendants, but janitors, mechanics, food services and all the other park workers. Thank You!
